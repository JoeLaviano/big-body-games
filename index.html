Schedule Heat Assignments

Eliot

2022 Placing: 3rd (of 5)
2021 Placing: 6th (of 7)
Against All Odds

There's a stench of inequity in the BBG, if you ask Eliot. For two straight years, winning more games has not equated to moving up the ranks, leaving Eliot with disappointing placements for two years in a row. With a cornhole masterclass at PKL this off-season, Eliot is truly ruffling some feathers - he's looking sharp and poised for a deep run in 2023.

Keys to Success: Win More, Lose Less, Break Ties

Eric

2022 Placing: 5th (of 5)
2021 Placing: 4th (of 7)
Go West Young Man

We've got another West Coast Big Body on our hands, as Eric is making moves and running out west just months before BBG2023. A change in scenery should suit Eric, and it may be exactly what he needs to overcome two middle-of-the-road showings in the last two tournaments. The time change will be no match for Eric, who will no doubt make a huge splash entering Long Beach. The key will be remembering his East Coast roots - if Eric can stay true to form, he should have no issues making waves and manifesting his destiny.

Keys to Success: Physical Contact, Hitting the King, Truly

Joe

2022 Placing: 1st (of 5)
2021 Placing: 1st (of 7)
Plausible Deniability

It's undeniable - no matter how (or by whom) the score is kept, Joe manages to find a way to top the leaderboard. He's a perfect 2-for-2 and is showing no signs of slowing down on his way to a perfect three-peat sweep. His off-season work ethic has been lackluster at best, and with proven weaknesses in previous years, he can definitely be bested. Who will capitalize on their opportunity? Or will Joe remain unbeaten and keep a clean sheet through his third straight year?

Keys to Success: Overconfidence, Mental Intimidation, Accurate Scorekeeping

Nick

2022 Placing: N/A
2021 Placing: 5th (of 7)
Hometown Hopeful

Abstinence from the 2022 BBG was just about the only thing Panarello abstained from in the last year, successfully spawning an offspring of his own just weeks after the last competition. With a year to hone his craft and develop a gameplan, will Panarello reclaim his Biggest Body title and more, or will the sleepless nights of fatherhood come back to haunt him? He's got home-field advantage this year and could be primed to make some big body moves with support from his fans.

Keys to Success: National Anthem, Girth, No Active Malpractice Cases

Picca

2022 Placing: 4th (of 5)
2021 Placing: 7th (of 7)
Business Operations

The man of many strategies, Picca has had a tough go executing in competition. After his 1st place finish in the first event of 2021, Picca has had a bottom 3 finish in every event since. He's now a Director in his own right, making professional and life commitments like we've never seen before; how can he use that to his advantage and improve on history?

Keys to Success: Strategize, Direct, Execute

Theo

2022 Placing: N/A
2021 Placing: 2nd (of 7)
Implied Consent

Our first big body to enter fatherhood, Theo missed last year's tournament to enjoy some quality family time at home. It's a tale as old as time - new dads in professional sports have a huge stride of momentum in their comeback season - Steph Curry, Kyle Rudolph, Derrick White to name a few. Will Theo be able to capitalize on his newly acquired dad strength? He's here to prove that while the Wonder Boy lifestyle may be gone and past, but he still has what it takes to come out on top.

Keys to Success: Stay Alive, Stay Awake, Stay on Top

Tim

2022 Placing: 2nd (of 5)
2021 Placing: 3rd (of 7)
All Work, All Play

If you were to genetically engineer the perfect BBG athlete, you'd essentially get Tim - he's got the look, he's got the talent, he's got the charisma. So ask yourself, why has Tim not been able to perform in competition? The bucket hat was a huge boost for Tim's confidence, but he'll need more than that to overcome the devastating defeats of the past. He's been practicing all off-season to claim his birthright; it's up to him to not thwart his opportunities and make a name for himself in year 3.

Keys to Success: Gimme, Timmy, Shimmy
