Mission Statement

The Big Body Games (BBG) are an event based upon three founding principles: athleticism, dexterity, and sportsmanship. This proven trifecta brings athletes together in a weekend-long, eventfilled tournament of backyard sports and healthy competition, with the goal of fostering both an aggressive athletic atmosphere and an environment founded on fraternity and camaraderie.
